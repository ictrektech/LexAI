# Contract Type Checklists

Use these checklists as review prompts. Do not treat them as legal conclusions without retrieved evidence.

## Sales / Purchase Contract

- Subject matter: name, model, quantity, quality standards, specifications.
- Price and payment: amount, tax, invoice, payment milestone, setoff, refund.
- Delivery: time, place, method, freight, risk/title transfer.
- Acceptance: inspection period, criteria, deemed acceptance, rejection/rework.
- Warranty: scope, period, remedy, spare parts, after-sales service.
- Breach: delay delivery/payment, quality defect, liability cap, liquidated damages.
- Termination: trigger, cure period, return/refund, transition.
- Attachments: quotation, PO, technical specs, acceptance form.

## Service Contract

- Scope of services, deliverables, milestones, service levels.
- Personnel qualification and subcontracting.
- Acceptance criteria and change management.
- Fees, expenses, invoices, taxes.
- Client cooperation obligations and delay consequences.
- IP ownership of deliverables and background IP license.
- Confidentiality, data/security requirements.
- Termination assistance, handover, post-termination obligations.

## Lease Contract

- Property identity, title/use right, permitted use.
- Rent, deposit, payment date, invoice, late fee.
- Lease term, delivery, handover condition.
- Repair/maintenance, decoration, restoration.
- Sublease/assignment restrictions.
- Early termination, renewal, relocation, force majeure.
- Liability for property damage, safety, compliance.

## Labor / Employment Contract

- Employer and employee qualification.
- Term, probation, position, workplace, working hours.
- Salary, social insurance, benefits, overtime.
- Confidentiality and non-compete: scope, term, compensation.
- Training service period and liquidated damages.
- Termination procedure and statutory restrictions.
- Employee handbook or policy incorporation.

## NDA / Confidentiality Agreement

- Confidential information definition and exclusions.
- Purpose limitation and recipient scope.
- Protection standard and permitted disclosure.
- Term and survival period.
- Return/destroy obligations.
- Remedies, injunctive relief, liquidated damages.
- Residual knowledge and reverse engineering restrictions.

## IP / Technology Contract

- Ownership of foreground/background IP.
- License scope: territory, term, exclusivity, sublicensing.
- Open-source, third-party materials, infringement warranty.
- Deliverables, source code, documentation, escrow.
- Improvement ownership and commercialization rights.
- Confidentiality, data/security, export controls if relevant.

## Equity / Investment / Cooperation Agreement

- Parties and authority.
- Investment amount, valuation, payment conditions.
- Conditions precedent and closing deliverables.
- Governance, reserved matters, veto rights.
- Representations and warranties.
- Founder restrictions, non-compete, equity transfer.
- Default, redemption/repurchase, liquidation preference.
- Dispute resolution and enforcement feasibility.
