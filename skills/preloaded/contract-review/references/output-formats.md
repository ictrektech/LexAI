## Business-Facing Summary

Use when the audience is business rather than legal:

- Start with "能不能签" style conclusion if evidence supports it.
- Limit legal jargon.
- Put the top risks and actions first.
- Keep detailed citations inline but avoid long legal explanation unless requested.
